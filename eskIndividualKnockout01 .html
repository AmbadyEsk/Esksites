ESK KNOVKOUT CHAMPIONSHIP - 1 Results

ESK UCL TOURNAMENT

Explore our tournament results to gain insights into our team's performance and achievements, showcasing a dynamic journey of competition and success.







The ESK Knockout Championship featured a total of 65 players representing different teams:

Aluet team: 21 players

Classix team: 16 players

Inuit team: 7 players

Unity team: 8 players

Yupik team: 13 players



After a series of knockout rounds, the tournament reached its semi-final stage with the following players:



Akshay Ambady from Classix

Noobmaster from Aleut

Gokul from Classix

Manoj from Inuit



In the semi-finals, Noobmaster emerged victorious against Akshay Ambady,

and Manoj secured a win against Gokul. The final match witnessed an

intense battle, with Manoj from the Inuit team ultimately

triumphing over Noobmaster from Aleut, claiming the title

of the ESK Knockout Championship.
