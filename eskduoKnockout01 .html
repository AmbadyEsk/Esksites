ESK DUO CHAMPIONSHIP

In this duo tournament, a total of 29 teams are set to compete for victory and glory.









ESK Duo Championship, a knockout-style tournament featuring a total of 29 pairs,

showcased the competitive spirit among various teams.

Esk Classix with 9 pairs,

Esk Aleut with 8 pairs,

Esk Yupik with 5 pairs,

Esk Inuit with 4 pairs,

and Esk Unity with 3 pairs

all demonstrated their prowess in the duo format.

ESK Duo Championship, a knockout-style tournament featuring 29 teams,
reached its pinnacle in a riveting final showdown. In the semifinals,
the dynamic duo of Akash and Gokul from Esk Classix triumphed over Fajas
and Supreme, also hailing from Esk Classix. Simultaneously,
Dkm and Cronus of Esk Unity secured their spot in the finals by defeating
Sinan and Ambady from Esk Classix in a fiercely contested match. The grand
finale witnessed a spectacular clash between Akash and Gokul against
Dkm and Cronus, with Akash and Gokul emerging victorious. The Esk
Classix team celebrated the championship as Akash and Gokul proudly
secured the title of ESK Duo Champions, showcasing their exceptional
teamwork and skill.
