<<<<<<< HEAD ======= In November 2020, a group of passionate football enthusiasts came together to form Team Mithra Pes Club (TMPC), marking the inception of a journey that would redefine virtual football gaming. Founded by members of the Soccer Galaxy Efootball Whatsapp Group, TMPC started with 15 members under the umbrella of the All Kerala Pes Association Registered Club (AKPA) clan. As time progressed, the club underwent significant transformations, not only in name and identity but also in its scope and achievements.

Evolution and Expansion:

By April 2021, TMPC evolved into FC ESKIMOS, accompanied by a change in logo and the establishment of two sub-teams: ESK INUIT and ESK YUPIK. The club's growth remained dynamic as it introduced a third sub-team, ESK ALEUT, in November 2021. The addition of new sub-teams reflected the organization's commitment to inclusivity and diversity within its gaming community.

Tournaments and Mergers:

FC ESKIMOS quickly made a mark in the gaming scene by hosting two all-India level tournaments in association with AKPA. These tournaments, held in December 2021 and May 2023, not only celebrated the club's anniversaries but also showcased the prowess of its members. The subsequent mergers with Efootball clans Tornadox and Pmt further strengthened FC ESKIMOS' position in the gaming community.

Continued Growth:

In June 2023, FC ESKIMOS announced the addition of two more sub-teams, ESK CLASSIX and ESK UNITY, bringing the total number of sub-teams to five. This expansion mirrored the club's commitment to providing diverse opportunities for its members to engage in competitive gameplay and foster a sense of unity.
