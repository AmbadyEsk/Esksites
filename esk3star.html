ESK 3Star Challenge

The Esk 3Star Challenge, with an impressive roster of 85 participants, ignited fierce competition as players from various disciplines converged for a thrilling tournament.

The ESK STAR CHALLENGE brought together 85 skilled players in a thrilling individual esports tournament. The competitors were divided into five teams: Aleut, Classix, Inuit, Unity, and Yupik. Each team showcased their talents, with 16 groups in the first stage, consisting of 5 players each.The first stage featured 16 groups, each comprising 5 players.
